ABOUT ME:

I have completed my graduation in Metallurgical Engineering in 2018 and post graduation in Masters in Material Engineering in 2023 from NED university of Engineering and technology. Additionally, I am also certified from the Failure analysis center (institute of Space Technology, Pakistan) in Industrial Failure Analysis and Prevention. Currently, I have been working in SGS Pakistan private limited since 2018, as a Senior Inspection Engineer with more than 4 year of experience mainly in oil/gas, power generation, and fertilizers industries. I am an American Petroleum certified Inspector API-571, Damage Mechanisms Affecting Fixed Equipment in the Refining Industry. My diverse background includes valuable expertise in root cause failure analysis of Mechanical and Metallurgical Failure, Risk-Based Inspection, planning for inspection, Material Testing, Corrosion and coating testing. I have provided me with a comprehensive skill set I have contributed to impacting business outcomes through effective organization, prioritization, and execution of key projects. My skills and qualifications are an ideal match to the research and quality support requirements.
